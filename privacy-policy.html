Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 June 2026

ReadEasy ("we", "our", or "the app") is a web application for combining EPUB files and for reading, editing, and saving articles. This policy explains what information the app handles, why, and your choices. It applies to https://merge-epubs.vercel.app/ and the companion ReadEasy browser extension.

Information we collect

  • Google account profile. When you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, profile picture, and your Google account identifier (the OpenID sub). We use Google Identity Services and request only basic profile and email scopes.
  • Saved articles. If you save an article, the article's title, source URL, site name, timestamps, and the article's HTML content (including any edits you make) are stored so you can access them later across devices.
  • EPUB files you upload for merging. Files you submit to the Combine EPUBs feature are processed to produce the combined output.

How we use your information

  • To sign you in and display your account name and avatar.
  • To store and retrieve the articles you choose to save, scoped privately to your Google account.
  • To combine the EPUB files you submit and return the merged file to you.

We do not sell your information, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties for their own purposes.

Where your data is stored

  • Saved articles are stored in our Supabase project (database and file storage), partitioned by your Google account identifier so that only you can access them.
  • EPUB merging is performed by our processing service. Uploaded files are processed in memory to generate the combined EPUB and are not retained on the server after the response is returned.
  • Your sign-in session (your Google ID token and basic profile) is kept in your browser's local storage so you stay signed in. You can clear it at any time by signing out.

Data retention and deletion

  • Saved articles remain until you delete them. You can delete any saved article from the Library panel at any time, which removes both its database record and stored content.
  • Signing out clears your session from your browser.
  • To request deletion of all data associated with your account, contact us at the email below and we will remove your stored articles and account records.

Third-party services

The app relies on the following providers to function. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Google (Sign-In / OAuth) — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Supabase (database & storage) — supabase.com/privacy
  • Vercel (web hosting) — vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

We use Google user data only to provide the features described above and adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
